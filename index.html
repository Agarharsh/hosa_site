Being pre-health isn't necessarily a walk in the park, but we make it feel as close as possible to one!

By joining Pitt HOSA and inviting your friends, we establish the first comprehensive Pre-Health community of students at the University of Pittsburgh.

Who can join HOSA?

Anyone who is involved in or studying in a healthcare field can join HOSA! That includes...



Dentists

Neurologists


Nutritionists

Pharmacists


Anesthesiologists

Physical Therapists
